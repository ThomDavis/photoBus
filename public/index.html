  • 2009-2011

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Nearly 10 years ago Ruby the wander bus came into our lives. It had always been a dream of mine to own a VW bus so when I saw that Ruby was for sale I convinced my husband Thom to make my dream come true. Ruby came to us in wonderful condition. She has been loved by many over the years. Ruby has had a few jobs along the way. She has been a delivery bus for Meals on Wheels and her previous owner used her for floral deliveries. She never fails to bring a smile to everyone who sees her. This year we have begun transitioning her into a photo booth and we can’t wait to share her with all of you.

It usually starts like this... “What year is your bus?” And then just like I’ve made a new friend. Sometimes we talk for minutes, most of the time it’s longer. Lots of people ask questions and it’s incredible to see them light up over something as simple as an old car. The ones I really love, the ones that warm my heart, are the people that have bus stories of their own. Just recently I met a wonderful gentleman who had been stationed in Germany in the 70’s. He told me all about the bus he had and how he regretted not being able to bring her back to the states with him. He squeezed my shoulder, told me to take good care of her, and went about his day. Owning Ruby has opened the door to an entire family of VW lovers and I am always amazed by their words of kindness and encouragement. Do you have a bus story? I’d love to hear it.
